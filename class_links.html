Places To Hang Out With Friends

On this page, I have listed a few places you can go when you hang out with your friends.

Number One:

This is simple, but you could go to each other's houses.

Number Two:

You could go to Corte Madera Mall. There are two sides: Town Center, and The Village. You can get food, shop, or just walk around.

Number Three:

You could watch a movie at the Larkspur Landing Cinema, The Lark Theater, or The Fairfax Theater.
